Hi I'm Penny

I'm a proud mum and wife, a pastor and qualified primary teacher. In each of these roles I am passionate about unlocking the full potential in others. This desire caused me to train as a Strengths Coach which I believe is a very effective tool for seeing both individuals and teams develop to their full potential.

Bringing experience from the education sector and sports and health arena, I am passionate about seeing people reach their full potential by understanding the amazing gifts and strengths they have, and how these can be used effectively in different spheres of life. I believe life should be enjoyed and not endured, and using my strengths of Communication, Woo, Includer, Connectedness and Self-Assurance I love to help people unlock their strengths and leave with a new found confidence in who they have been created to be. It has been said that it is the brave who are free, and I love helping people who are willing to bravely consider their own lives , find a new level of freedom in understanding themselves and how they can effectively use their strengths in every day life.

I am qualified to coach individuals for both their top 5 and full 34 strengths.
