What is strengths coaching?

Strengths coaching is all about unlocking your inner potential by identifying
and developing your personal strengths. The coaching is personalized to your unique
strengths to help you understand how you are wired and use that to maximize your life!

Using the Clifton StrengthsFinder tools Penny empowers you to discover your areas of talent
and helps you to effectively understand, develop and maximise these areas so they become strengths.

The first step is to complete the Gallup online Strengths Finder assessment, which is comprised of
180 questions. This will identify your Top 5 strengths and Penny will then personally coach you,
helping you understand each of these strengths to a greater extent and showing you how they can be
used together to help you to flourish and achieve maximum results in every area of your life.


Business

Understand and build on your top strengths to excel in the workplace and to maximise your capacity and efficiency.

Achieve

Rather than spending your valuable time trying to fix your weaknesses, focus on developing your strengths and using them to help you be successful.

Maximise your development, by understanding and honing your strengths. Then use this to help those around you understand how you function most effectively.

Unique

You are incredibly unique. The chance someone else has the same Top 5 Strengths as you is 1 in 278,000, and in the same order as you is 1 in 33 million.

Know yourself

Gain a greater understanding of who you are, and be equipped with a language to express it.

Grow

Develop and amplify what is best in you, and apply that effectively in all areas of your life.
